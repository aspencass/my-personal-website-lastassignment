Hello there! Thanks for visiting my site.

My name is Cassidy Wagner, it's nice to e-meet you.

Cassidy is a Colorado native with a background as a dance artist. She danced professionally across Europe and the Middle East after receiving a degree in Dance from New York University. Since 2019 she's immeresed herself in studying the Gaga Movement Language with Ohad Naharin, which involved living in Tel Aviv pre-pandemic. She now teaches a weekly Sunday Gaga class in Denver and produces collaborative contemporary dance performances in local galleries downtown as part of the Santa Fe Art District's First Friday Art Walks. These evenings explore movement improvisation exercises and consciously delegate creative freedom to the performers and dancers involved. Simple pleasures include driving with the windows down, coffee, plants, breakfast burritos, dancing, and now coding!
